Privacy Policy

Gravitas: Space Puzzles ("the App") is developed by Anton Brekhov ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information ourselves. The App does not require an account and does not have a backend server.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the App displays interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data including device identifiers (IDFA / advertising ID), IP address, and coarse location to serve personalised or contextual ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalised Ads.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Gravitas Premium" purchase processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment details. Purchase records are managed by your Apple ID.

Local Storage

The App stores your level progress and premium status locally on your device using iOS shared preferences. This data never leaves your device.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
